Welcome to MyBookShelf!

MyBookShelf is an interactive and fun book app where you can personalize your reading experience! Maybe you want a good book suggestion, or perhaps you have a favorite book that you want to tell others about, or maybe you want to join the discussion about the latest bookclub read! Whatever your literary desires may be, MyBookShelf is here to satisfy the bookworm in you! Sign up to create your own "shelf" and get updates with the book of the month, and to join our virtual book club.
